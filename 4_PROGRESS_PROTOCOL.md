## üìù INSTRUCTIONS FOR FILLING OUT

**Every Week:**
1. Take photos (same lighting, same time, same location)
2. Rate your skin (1-10 scale)
3. Track product usage (checkboxes)
4. Note lifestyle factors (sleep, sun, diet)
5. Record any reactions or issues
6. Update inventory
7. Set goals for next week

**Every 2 Weeks (Recommended):**
- Compare photos side-by-side
- Calculate score improvement
- Adjust routine if needed
- Celebrate wins!

**Monthly:**
- Review long-term trends
- Compare to expected timeline
- Decide if adding Tretinoin (see dermatologist)
